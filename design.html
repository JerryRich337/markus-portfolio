Ferrari x Liberty Walk

A concept marketing campaign for Ferrari in collaboration with Liberty Walk, a Japanese automotive company known for its extravagant body kits and modifications. The campaign aimed to blend Ferrari's luxury and performance with Liberty Walk's bold and aggressive styling, creating a unique fusion of elegance and street culture. The visuals showcased a Ferrari model transformed with Liberty Walk's signature wide-body kit, set against urban backdrops that highlighted the car's striking presence. The campaign included posters and social media graphics to capture the attention of car enthusiasts and the general public alike.
